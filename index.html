Hello! We are team 18209 Full Stop, a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team based in Rhode Island.

What we do

As a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team, we build robots to compete against other schools in high-energy robotics competitions.

We work to build, design, and code a robot while promoting STEM awareness both in our community and on social media.
