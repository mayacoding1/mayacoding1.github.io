All seasons!

We have been a team since 2020, and have competed in numerous events every year!

Decode

The 2025-2026 season has been our strongest season yet. We expanded our team to 10 members and participated in new events!

Awards: Winning Alliance Captain (Qualifier), Think Award, Control Award

Competitions: Rhode Island State Qualifier, Rhode Island State Championship, New England Premier Event (Crock Division)

Into the Deep

The 2024-2025 season was a rebuilding year for our team, as many of our previous members had graduated. This still was a highly successful year, and we learned numerous valuable lessons as a team.

Awards: Motivate Award

Competitions: Rhode Island State Qualifier, Rhode Island State Championship

Centerstage

For the 2023-2024 season, we returned to our roots by reuniting as one larger team.

Awards: Judges' Choice Award

Competitions: Rhode Island State Qualifier, Rhode Island State Championship

Powerplay

For the 2022-2023 season, we changed up our structure by splitting into two smaller teams: 18209 Full Stop and 22671 Ellipsis.

Awards: Think Award

Competitions: Rhode Island State Qualifier, Rhode Island State Championship

Freight Frenzy

For the 2021-2022 season, we expanded our team to 14 members and competed in person for the first time.

Competitions: Rhode Island State Championship
