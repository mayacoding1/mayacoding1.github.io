Our values

Our team aligns with core FIRST values of gracious professionalism and coopertition. We aim to be a fun, inclusive, and welcoming team that can work well with others both in and outside of the competition.

We love meeting other teams, comparing game strategies, and helping others to keep competitions fun and competitive!

Our school

We are proud to be members of South Kingstown High School, an innovative and top-ranked high school in Rhode Island.

We are part of our school's Career and Technical Education (CTE) Program for computer science, which helps introduce engineering concepts through hands-on experiences.
